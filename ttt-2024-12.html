🎛️ .vimrc (Dotfiles)

Dotfiles are configuration files used by various software programs to customize their behavior for a specific user. They are typically hidden files (the dot at the beginning of their name makes them hidden by default in Unix-like systems) and are stored in the user's home directory. Dotfiles are often used for shell configurations, application settings, and other preferences that a user may want to modify.

" Ignore case of searches
set ignorecase
" Highlight dynamically as pattern is typed
